About Indonesian Sphalerite

Sphalerite is a zinc sulfide mineral that is the chief ore of zinc. It consists largely of zinc sulfide in crystalline form but almost always contains variable iron.

Indonesian sphalerite is particularly prized for its exceptional clarity and unique color variations, ranging from yellow to deep red and black, depending on its iron content.

Key Characteristics:

  • Hardness: 3.5-4 on Mohs scale
  • Refractive Index: 2.37-2.43
  • Specific Gravity: 3.9-4.1

Indonesian Gemstone Heritage

Mining in Indonesia

Indonesia has a rich history of gemstone mining, with sphalerite deposits primarily found in Java and Sumatra. Our gemstones are ethically sourced from small-scale mines that prioritize environmental sustainability.

The volcanic geology of Indonesia creates ideal conditions for the formation of high-quality sphalerite crystals with exceptional clarity and color.
